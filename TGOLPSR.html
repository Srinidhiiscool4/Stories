Some claim her as wailing woman (crying with grief, pain, and anger); others claim her as a woman who lost her husband / son or sometimes she lost both her husband and son. Here we discuss about Lola, the woman who disappeared in search of her son. There was a Ski resort located in La Parva town and its famous mountain spot lies in Chilean Andes (border between Chile and Argentina and include the highest section of the mountain range). Most of the people who live in the La Parva town knew the woman Lola before she disappears.

Entering The Cave

According to study, the woman Lola dated pro skier drew tabke the owner of the restaurant. The hidden story of Lola disappeared was narrated by both the owner and the person who doing patrol work near the ski mountain areas. As per investigation, it was a peak ski season (winter season with heavy snow). During this season fogs will be thick in the air where we not able to figure out the person standing next to us. Lola and her younger son went to the slope of the mountain (Andes) and decided to spend some time in peace over there. As time passes, thick fog started to rose up which in turn gives the prediction of heavy storm. So, Lola and her son decided to get down from the slope. As they were moving back, Lola last communication with her son and she couldn’t find him. She started shouting her son’s name and accidently slipped from the slope of the mountain. She got herself hit heavily and stuck between the rocks. Lift operator while passes by encountered the woman. First, he thought that she was dead and later he realized that she was alive moaning her son’s name. He tried giving her first aid by taking her to his cabin. After that he ran to fetch a doctor, to give proper treatment for her. Once the doctor and the lift operator get back, they haven’t found the woman on the bed just the blood strains left over the blankets.

After that, they couldn’t find neither the woman nor her son. But till today in the lift cabin, they can hear different sounds, along with the scream of Lola who is searching for her son. They couldn’t differentiate the sound of wind nor Lola scream especially during winter.
